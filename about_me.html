My Methodology

I believe the engineering mindset is not limited to technical problems, it’s a way of approaching everyday life. At its core, engineering combines math, science, logic, and creativity to solve problems. You start with an idea, test it, identify what works and what doesn’t, and then refine it until the result is effective and reliable. This process goes into everything I do, whether I’m designing a product or cooking a meal.

When I design something, I consider its form, function, and structure together. How does it look? What is it meant to do? How does it work? A good solution shouldn’t just function correctly, it should feel right to the person using it. By keeping the user’s needs, comfort, and experience in mind, I aim to create designs that are not only functional, but also intuitive and desirable.
